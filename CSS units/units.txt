Absolute Units:

px (pixel)
pt (point)
in (inch)
cm (centimeter)
mm (millimeter)
pc (pica)
Relative Units:

em (relative to the font-size of the element)
rem (relative to the font-size of the root element)
vw (relative to 1% of the viewport's width)
vh (relative to 1% of the viewport's height)
vmin (relative to the smaller dimension of the viewport)
vmax (relative to the larger dimension of the viewport)
Percentage Units:

% (percentage)
Other Units:

ch (character width)
ex (ex-height of the current font)
fr (fractional unit, used in CSS Grid Layout)
